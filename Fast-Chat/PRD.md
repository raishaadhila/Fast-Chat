# 🚀 AI-Powered CRM Automation SaaS

## 🎯 Vision
Empower small businesses to automate customer relationship management using AI, without needing technical skills or expensive software.

## 🎨 Key Features
1. **Omnichannel messaging:** Integrate with WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram to manage all customer conversations in one place
2. **AI-powered responses:** Automatically respond to common customer questions using OpenAI and Anthropic Claude models
3. **Personalized tone:** Tailor AI responses to match the brand voice and tone of each business
4. **Customer profiles:** Build rich customer profiles with conversation history, preferences, and purchase data
5. **Analytics dashboard:** Visualize key metrics like response times, satisfaction scores, and agent performance

## 🎯 Target Users
- Small business owners who manage customer relationships manually
- Startups who need a low-cost, easy-to-use CRM solution
- Businesses who want to offer 24/7 customer support without hiring more staff

## 🏆 Goals
- Acquire 10 paying customers within 3 months of launch
- Achieve 90% customer satisfaction rating within 6 months
- Reduce average customer response time to under 1 minute
